
Originally Answered: Since the CDC announced COVID immunity only lasts 3 months, will the immunity from the vaccines in development last any longer?


They didn’t say that, and it can’t be true.

If immunity lasted only three months for most people we would be seeing a wave of thousands of second-time infections in Italy, Spain, the UK, France, and New York by now from the nearly million people who had tested positive in those places by the end of April.

We aren’t.
We are seeing a tiny handful of probable laboratory errors in Spain, and no symptomatic reinfections.

We know that it lasts at least three months.
In fact, given essentially no reinfections after three months, it almost certainly lasts at least six months for almost everybody.
