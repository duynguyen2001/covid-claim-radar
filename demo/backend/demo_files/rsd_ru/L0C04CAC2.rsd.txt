The scientist stated that the coronavirus existed dormant even before the outbreak in Wuhan.
Tom Jefferson, a senior researcher at the Oxford Centre for Evidence Medicine, stated that the new type of COVID-19 coronavirus existed long before the outbreak in Wuhan.
The Daily Telegraph reports.
He noted that the coronavirus was dormant and that it was caused by a combination of natural factors.
According to the scientist, traces of coronavirus infection were found in Barcelona sewage collected in March 2019.
"I think the virus was already here, and by" here "I mean" everywhere, "" said Jefferson.
The scientist added that COVID-19 traces were also found in samples from Turin and Milan, which were obtained in December 2019.
He also said that the virus does not come out of nowhere, it "is always here and sometimes something activates it."
On 31 December 2019, Chinese authorities announced an outbreak of pneumonia in the city of Wuhan.
The cause of the disease is a new type of SARS-CoV-2 coronavirus that causes COVID-19 disease.
On 11 March, the World Health Organization (WHO) announced the outbreak of a new coronavirus pandemic.
