On 31 December 2019, Chinese authorities announced an outbreak of pneumonia in the city of Wuhan.
The cause of the disease is a new type of coronavirus, officially named COVID-19.
According to the latest data, more than 133,000 people were infected, 4,967 people were killed, and 70,352 were cured.
