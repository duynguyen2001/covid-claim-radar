Israelis will try to treat marijuana from coronavirus
Patients receiving treatment for the deadly Chinese coronavirus COVID-19, whose pandemic has been terrorizing the world for a month, will receive medical cannabis at the Tel Aviv Medical Center "Ikilov."
For example, experimental treatment will be given to moderate patients, reports The Times of Israel.
Doctors want to take advantage of the antiviral properties of the active substance cannabis to slow down the development of the virus in the body.
