"Coronavirus first appeared not in China": experts voiced a new version
Public Domain
Coronavirus did not originate in Wuhan, although it was originally discovered there, according to Chinese experts.
Zeng Guan, a staff member of the China Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), suggested that the infection had reached Wuhan through imported seafood and meat.
According to the expert, Beijing has evidence that COVID-19 appeared in Italy a few months before the first case of infection in Wuhan, reports South China Morning Post
In October, human coronavirus was also found for the first time in China on a package of frozen imported cod.
