Media: China's first case of coronavirus detected in November
Moscow, 13 Mar - RIA News.
The first case of a new type of coronavirus in China was discovered as early as 17 November, rather than in December, as previously thought, reports the South China Morning Post with reference to government documents.
According to the publication, he is a 55-year-old resident of Hubei Province, where the outbreak of coronavirus spread.
In November alone, nine carriers of coronavirus were identified in the province, but none were confirmed as "zero patient" - the first infected person to spread the epidemic.
Around 120,000 people in more than 100 countries have been infected with coronavirus, most have recovered, but more than 4,000 have died.
On 11 March, the World Health Organization announced the outbreak of a coronavirus pandemic.
