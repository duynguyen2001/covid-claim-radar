Moscow authorities reminded of the need to wear masks on the dance floor
The Moscow authorities banned the work of night establishments from 27 January.
Until then, clubs, bars, restaurants and karaoke could not work between 23: 00 and 6: 00.
The restriction was imposed against the background of the worsening situation of the coronavirus from 13 November to 15 January, and was subsequently extended.
The minimum number of coronavirus infections since the end of September is 1.8 thousand.
