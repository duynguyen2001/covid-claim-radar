China's first case of coronavirus was identified in November
A 55-year-old man from Hubei Province, China, is believed to be the first person to have contracted a new type of coronavirus in November 2019.
The Hong Kong edition of the South China Morning Post reports this with reference to unpublished Chinese government documents.
In China, they decided that the peak of the coronavirus epidemic had passed.

The virus was detected in the patient on 17 November.
Doctors later reported one to five cases per day, but it was not until the end of the year that doctors realized they were dealing with a new type of coronavirus.
On 15 December, 27 people were infected with COVID-19, and on 20 December, 60.
As of 2019, there were 266 cases of coronavirus in China.
